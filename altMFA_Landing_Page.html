What's Included
✍️ Twice Weekly

Write

Each week includes two writing components designed to build a real, sustained practice.

Wednesday Morning · 7:30–8:30AM

Writing Session

A short, heads-down block of protected writing time. No feedback, no agenda. Just you and other writers, writing.

Wednesday Evening · 7:00–9:00PM

Writing Workshop

A structured small-group workshop where you share drafts, give and receive feedback, and work on revision.

📚 Once Weekly

Read

Reading like a writer is a skill in itself. Each week, we study two essays through four lenses.

The readings include canonical and contemporary writers — Virginia Woolf, Frederick Douglass, and Henry David Thoreau, alongside Zadie Smith, Paul Graham, and Henrik Karlsson.

Perception What does this writer notice that most people miss?
Valuation Why do they care, and how do they make it matter?
Position Where do they stand, and how did they earn that ground?
Expression How does the way they write reinforce what they're saying?

The obvious answer was grad school

We both wanted dedicated time to work on our own writing, to study craft seriously, and to do it alongside people who cared as much as we did. Between the cost, the time, and the career interruption, grad school wasn't a real option. So we're building a program that brings it to life — without asking you to put yours on hold.
